KON BANEGA CROREPATI

Artist: Amitabh Bachchan

Genre: Game/Quiz Show

Description: Kaun Banega Crorepati (KBC) is a popular Indian television quiz show hosted by Bollywood legend Amitabh Bachchan. Contestants answer a series of multiple-choice questions based on general knowledge and current affairs to win cash prizes. The show offers lifelines like "Audience Poll" and "Phone a Friend" to assist contestants. Its engaging format and inspirational stories have made it a household favorite. KBC not only entertains but also emphasizes the value of knowledge and perseverance.!
