YEH RISTA KYA KEHLATA HAI

Artist: Akshara Maheshwari Singhania - Hina Khan, Naitik Singhania - Karan Mehra, Naira Singhania Goenka - Shivangi Joshi, Kartik Goenka - Mohsin Khan, Akshara Goenka - Pranali Rathod.

Genre: Family Show

Description: Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai is one of India’s longest-running television dramas. It originally centered around the lives of Akshara and Naitik, depicting their journey through love, marriage, and family. Over the years, the show has introduced multiple generations, focusing on complex family relationships and societal values. Its current storyline follows Abhimanyu and Akshara’s journey, dealing with themes of love, separation, and redemption. Known for its relatable characters and emotional storytelling, the show has maintained its popularity since its debut in 2009.!
